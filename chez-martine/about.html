About us

The story

"Chez Martine", which can be translated as “At Martine’s house”,
is a French bakery created in 2012 in Paris, France, by Stéphane Boulanger.

It is his mother, Martine, who gave him the taste for food and,
more particularly, a passion for desserts.
Martine used to cook every day for the family, and each meal ended with a homemade
dessert that had the unexplainable taste of a mother's love for her children.

It is this sweet memory, which he always compares to a hug on a cloud,
that motivated him throughout the process of creating his bakery.

The goal is that each person tasting his desserts feels the same
pleasure he himself felt throughout his childhood.

With the desire to share this feeling to the world, Stéphane
arrived in Canada in 2020 and opened his boutique in downtown Toronto.

The raw material

The products of "Chez Martine" are manufactured using quality raw materials. For example, for the base of the products, Stéphane uses Nunweiler's flour,
and the President butter, a French brand, but made right here in Canada.
In addition, he uses Valrhona's chocolate, that he imports directly from France.

With ingredients of this quality, we can assure you that your taste buds will enjoy every bite of our products.
